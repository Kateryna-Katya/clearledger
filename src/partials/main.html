About the Game

Arcade chaos with a sweet twist

Look, Candy Carnival Rush isn't reinventing the wheel here, but it nails the endless runner formula with some seriously eye-catching visuals. The carnival setting creates a sense of constant motion and excitement, while gameplay stays simple and intuitive. What keeps me coming back is how each run throws different stuff at you - you never quite know what's around the corner.
